It could look like requesting permission, applying for a grant or funding, trying to opinioins on something or it could be more drastic/critical changes like at work with potential big impact. Even in those cases I think there's a variation of doing those things in a "safe" way. A lot of time people won't believe or see value in things/ideas until in front of them or done. 

Hence, my motivation for this. By safe, it could look like a scaled down version that achieves a little part of the goal. Or at least sufficient enough to serve as proof of concept.
